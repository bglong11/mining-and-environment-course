Mining and Environmental Impacts

An Online Course Exploring the Lifecycle Impacts of Mining

Welcome to the Course!

This course provides a comprehensive overview of the various environmental and social impacts associated with mining activities. We will explore these impacts throughout the entire mine lifecycle, from initial exploration and development to final closure and site rehabilitation. Our aim is to foster a deeper understanding of the challenges and the best practices for responsible environmental management in the mining sector.

The lessons are designed to be interactive and build upon concepts found in "Mining and the Environment," supplemented with real-world examples and current information.
